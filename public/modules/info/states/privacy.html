Epollo Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or upload to https://epollo.herokuapp.com/ (the “Epollo”).

When we talk about “uploaded” or “uploaded data” or “uploading” in this Privacy Policy, we are talking both about stories, and threads.

Personal information we collect

When you visit Epollo, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. Additionally, as you browse Epollo, we collect information about the individual web pages or stories that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to Epollo, and information about how you interact with Epollo. We refer to this automatically-collected information as “Device Information.”

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • “Cookies” are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous unique identifier. For more information about cookies, and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org.
  • “Log files” track actions occurring on Epollo, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
  • “Web beacons,” “tags,” and “pixels” are electronic files used to record information about how you browse Epollo.

Additionally when you sign up or upload anything to Epollo we collect certain information from you, including your first name, last name, email address. We refer to this information as “User Information.”

When we talk about “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy, we are talking both about Device Information and User Information.

How do we use your personal information?

We use the User Information that we collect generally to fulfill any uploading to Epollo (including processing your User Informations, and Device Informations). Additionally, we use this User Information to:

  • Communicate with you
  • Screen our uploaded data for potential risk or fraud
  • Show your name, username in Epollo
  • When in line with the preferences you have shared with us, provide you with information or advertising relating to our services.

We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential risk and fraud (in particular, your IP address), and more generally to improve and optimize our site (for example, by generating analytics about how our users browse and interact with the Site).

Sharing your personal information

We do not share your Personal Information with any third parties.

We use your personal information to display your uploaded data, for exaple: stories, threads, and user information for your profile page, exluding your email, which is never shared by Epollo.

Your rights

You have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information below.

Data retention

When you upload to Epollo, we will maintain your User Information for our records unless and until you ask us to delete this information.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

Changes are automatically accepted by you if you continue to use Epollo.

Contact us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at martinkondor@gmail.com.

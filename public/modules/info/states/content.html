Epollo Content Policy

Epollo is a platform for everyone to write, read, and share knowledge. The nature of it's content might be funny, serious, offensive, or anywhere in between. Unwelcome content

While Epollo generally provides a lot of leeway in what content is acceptable, here are some guidelines for content that is not. Please keep in mind the spirit in which these were written, and know that looking for loopholes is a waste of time.
Content is prohibited if it
  • Is illegal
  • Is involuntary pornography
  • Is sexual or suggestive content involving minors
  • Encourages or incites violence
  • Threatens, harasses, or bullies or encourages others to do so
  • Is personal and confidential information
  • Impersonates someone in a misleading or deceptive manner
  • Uses Epollo to solicit or facilitate any transaction or gift involving certain goods and services
  • Is spam

Prohibited behavior
In addition to not submitting unwelcome content, the following behaviors are prohibited on Epollo
  • Asking for votes or engaging in vote manipulation
  • Breaking Epollo or doing anything that interferes with normal use of Epollo
  • Creating multiple accounts to evade punishment or avoid restrictions

If a user of Epollo does something that goes against our Content Policy, the moderators of Epollo can do the following in order with the user.
  • Asking you nicely to knock it off
  • Asking you less nicely
  • Temporary or permanent suspension of accounts
  • Removal of privileges from, or adding restrictions to, accounts
  • Adding restrictions to share content
  • Removal of content
  • Banning from Epollo
